The Cancercelllines genomic information resource contains genome profiling data, somatic mutation information and associated metadata for thousands of human cancer cell lines. Originally spun out of the Progenetix oncogenomic resource with its collection of more than 100â€™000 individual genomic copy number screening datasets, cancercelllines.org provides an entry point for genomic variants in cell lines as well as for the exploration of related publications.

_cancercelllines.org_ is implemented on top of a [`bycon`](http://bycon.progenetix.org)
software stack with full support for the [Beacon v2](http://docs.genomebeacons.org)
GA4GH standard.
